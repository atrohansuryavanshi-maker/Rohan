Key Challenges in Immigration Consulting

Visa consultants face unique operational challenges that require specialized solutions

Long Processing Timelines

Visa applications can take months or even years, requiring meticulous tracking and client updates throughout the process.

Document-Heavy Workflows

Managing hundreds of documents per client including passports, diplomas, work experience, and financial proofs.

Multiple Visa Categories

Clients often apply for multiple visa types (PR, work, student, tourist) requiring different processes and documentation.

High Follow-up Dependency

Continuous client communication, document requests, and deadline reminders are critical for successful applications.
