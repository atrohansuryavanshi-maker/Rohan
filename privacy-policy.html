5. Telecalling CRM – Data Collection & Usage

Our Telecalling CRM collects specific data to optimize call center operations:

Key Assurance:

All call recordings are encrypted, access is strictly role-based, and we never sell data to third parties.

Data Collected:
  • Call Logs: Date, time, duration, and phone numbers
  • Call Recordings: Audio recordings (when enabled by organization policy)
  • Agent Performance: Call metrics, conversion rates, handling times
  • Lead Information: Contact details, lead source, interaction history
  • Call Dispositions: Outcome categorization and notes
Usage Purpose:
  • Optimize call center operations and routing
  • Quality assurance and training purposes
  • Performance analytics and reporting
  • Lead nurturing and follow-up automation
  • Compliance with telemarketing regulations

6. Task Management CRM – Data Handling

Our Task Management CRM handles project and task-related data:

Data Isolation:

Each organization's data is completely isolated. Data is used internally only for service improvement.

Data Collected:
  • Task Details: Titles, descriptions, deadlines, priorities
  • Project Information: Milestones, dependencies, timelines
  • Team Collaboration: Comments, file attachments, mentions
  • Time Tracking: Hours logged, time estimates vs. actual
  • Progress Analytics: Completion rates, bottlenecks, productivity metrics
Security Measures:
  • Role-based access controls for task visibility
  • End-to-end encryption for file attachments
  • Audit logs for all task modifications
  • Regular security vulnerability assessments
  • Data backup with 99.9% uptime guarantee

7. Overseas Education CRM – Student Data Protection

We treat student data with the highest level of confidentiality in our Overseas Education CRM:

Confidential Handling:

Limited staff access. No sharing with universities without explicit consent. Special protection for minor students.

Student Data Collected:
  • Personal Information: Full name, date of birth, contact details
  • Academic Records: Transcripts, certificates, test scores
  • Application Materials: SOPs, recommendation letters, portfolios
  • Visa Documentation: Passport details, financial documents, sponsorship letters
  • Communication Logs: Counselor-student interactions, university correspondence
Protection Measures:
  • End-to-end encryption for all student documents
  • Two-factor authentication for counselor access
  • Regular data protection training for staff
  • Data minimization - only essential data collected
  • Secure document sharing with expiration links

8. Field Service CRM – Location & Device Data

  • Service History: Job details, customer feedback, resolution notes
  • Customer Information: Service addresses, contact preferences
  • Inventory Data: Parts used, stock levels, equipment details
Usage Purpose:
  • Route optimization for field technicians
  • Real-time job status updates
  • Predictive maintenance scheduling
  • Customer satisfaction tracking
  • Service performance analytics

9. Sales CRM – Lead & Customer Data Usage

Our Sales CRM manages sales pipeline data with strict privacy controls:

  • Regular privacy impact assessments
